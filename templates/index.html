Oauth Contacts and calendar exporter

Secure, multi-tenant service for downloading Google Contacts and Calendar events (Google supported; Microsoft planned)

🏢 Multi-Tenant Architecture

Multiple users can authenticate independently and securely access their own Google Contacts without interference.

🔐 Secure Database Storage

SQLite database with AES encryption stores tokens securely. No contact data is ever saved on our servers.

🔑 Bearer Token Authentication

JWT-like access tokens provide secure API access with automatic token refresh and easy revocation.

📊 Multiple Export Formats

Download contacts in CSV or JSON and calendar events in ICS format. Great for backups and integrations.

🔒

Read-Only Access

This service only requests read-only access to your Google Contacts and Calendar. We cannot modify, delete, or add any data to your Google Account.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Start Authorization: Click "Get Started" to begin the OAuth flow.
  2. Authorize with Google: Grant read-only access to your contacts.
  3. Get Your Token: Copy the access token from the success page.
  4. Download Contacts: Use curl or integrate the API to retrieve your data.
