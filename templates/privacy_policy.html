PRIVACY POLICY

1. Information We Access and How We Use It

Our Service is designed to interact with your Google Account to provide you with contact download functionalities.

Google Account Data Access and Scope

When you connect your Google Account, you grant our Service permission to access specific information. We access this data solely for the purpose of operating the Service for you.

The specific data we may access includes:

  • Email Address: Your primary email address associated with your Google Account.
  • Profile Information (openid): Basic profile information (e.g., name, profile picture, locale) as provided by the Google OpenID Connect standard.
  • Google Contacts: Access to your personal Google Contacts list.

Crucially, our access to all of your Google information—including your Email, Profile, and Contacts—is strictly Read-Only. We cannot modify, delete, or add any data to your Google Account.

Purpose of Access

  • Contacts Access: The Service requires read-only access to your Google Contacts to allow you to download and export your contacts.
  • Email and Profile Access: This is used for user authentication, personalization, and to identify you as the Service user.

Data Storage and Retention

We do not save your personal contact list or any contact details on our servers.

We only store an authentication token provided by Google. This token is a secure, unique key that allows our Service to make authorized, read-only requests to Google's APIs on your behalf without us ever needing to store or see your actual Google password.

  • The Token: The token is encrypted using AES encryption and stored securely in our database. It is solely used to contact Google to access the explicitly permitted data points mentioned above.

2. Your Control and Data Revocation

Revocation of Access

You have the right to revoke our access to your Google Account data at any time.

  • You can disconnect your Google Account from our Service by revoking your access token through our API, or;
  • You can manage and revoke the permissions granted to Google Contacts Downloader by visiting your Google Security settings page at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Revoking access will immediately disable the Service's ability to access your Google Contacts, email, or profile information. If you revoke access, we will immediately delete the stored Google authentication token.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • All authentication tokens are encrypted using AES encryption before storage
  • Our database uses WAL mode for improved concurrent access and data integrity
  • We use HTTPS for all communications
  • Access tokens automatically expire and are cleaned up

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:
