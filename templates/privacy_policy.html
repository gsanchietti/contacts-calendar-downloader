PRIVACY POLICY

1. Information We Access and How We Use It

Our Service is designed to interact with your Google and Microsoft accounts to provide you with contact and calendar download functionalities.

Account Data Access and Scope

When you connect your account, you grant our Service permission to access specific information. We access this data solely for the purpose of operating the Service for you.

The specific data we may access includes:

  • Email Address: Your primary email address associated with your account.
  • Profile Information: Basic profile information (e.g., name, profile picture).
  • Contacts: Access to your personal contacts list.
  • Calendar: Access to your calendar events.

Crucially, our access to all of your information—including your Email, Profile, Contacts, and Calendar—is strictly Read-Only. We cannot modify, delete, or add any data to your account.

Purpose of Access

  • Contacts Access: The Service requires read-only access to your contacts to allow you to download and export them.
  • Calendar Access: The Service requires read-only access to your calendar to allow you to download and export events.
  • Email and Profile Access: This is used for user authentication, personalization, and to identify you as the Service user.

Data Storage and Retention

We do not save your personal contact list, calendar events, or any contact details on our servers.

We only store an authentication token provided by your OAuth provider. This token is a secure, unique key that allows our Service to make authorized, read-only requests on your behalf without us ever needing to store or see your actual password.

  • The Token: The token is encrypted using AES encryption and stored securely in our database. It is solely used to contact your provider's APIs to access the explicitly permitted data points mentioned above.

2. Your Control and Data Revocation

Revocation of Access

You have the right to revoke our access to your account data at any time.

  • You can disconnect your account from our Service by using the management URL provided after authorization, or;
  • You can manage and revoke the permissions granted through your provider's security settings page.

Revoking access will immediately disable the Service's ability to access your contacts, calendar, email, or profile information. If you revoke access, we will immediately delete the stored authentication token.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • All authentication tokens are encrypted using AES encryption before storage
  • Our database uses WAL mode for improved concurrent access and data integrity
  • We use HTTPS for all communications
  • Access tokens automatically expire and are cleaned up

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Communication with your provider's APIs using your encrypted authentication token to fetch your data
  • No third-party analytics or tracking services are used

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
