üîí Why Do Tokens Expire?

Export tokens have expiration dates for security reasons:

  • Data Protection: Limits exposure if a URL is accidentally shared
  • Access Control: Ensures you have control over who can access your data
  • Privacy: Reduces the risk of long-term unauthorized access

üí° What You Can Do

1. Create a New Token:

You can easily create a new export token through the OAuth flow. This will give you fresh URLs that work immediately.

2. Choose Your Expiration:

When creating a new token, you can select how long it should remain valid (7, 30, 90, or 365 days).

3. Save Your URLs:

After creating a token, bookmark or save the export URLs for easy access during the validity period.
